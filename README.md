# Product Sales Data Analysis

## Overview
This repository provides a comprehensive analysis of product sales data, focusing on extracting key business insights to drive informed decision-making. The project involves data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization to understand sales performance across different product types, locations, and pricing strategies.

## Features
*   **Data Cleaning:** Handles missing values and standardizes column names for consistent analysis.
*   **Sales Performance Analysis:** Visualizes the number of products sold and revenue generated by product type.
*   **Geographical Revenue Analysis:** Explores revenue distribution across various locations.
*   **Price Distribution:** Analyzes the distribution of product prices to understand pricing strategies and identify outliers.
*   **Interactive Visualizations:** Uses `matplotlib` and `seaborn` for clear and insightful data representation.

## Key Insights
*   **Skincare** consistently emerges as the top-performing product type, both in terms of units sold and revenue generated.
*   **Mumbai** and **Kolkata** are identified as the leading locations for revenue generation.
*   The **price distribution** of products is broad, suggesting a diverse product catalog with varied pricing tiers.
